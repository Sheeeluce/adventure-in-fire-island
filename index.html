About the trip to Fire Island

Last summer I took a trip to Fire Island, a long, sandy island just off the coast of Long Island. The ferry ride over felt like crossing into a quieter world where there are no cars and the air smells like salt and sunscreen. One of the highlights of the trip was walking along the wide beaches at sunset, watching the sky turn orange and pink over the Atlantic Ocean. I also explored the boardwalks that wind through the dunes and visited the historic Fire Island Lighthouse, climbing to the top for an amazing view of the ocean and the bay. In the evenings, we ate fresh seafood at small seaside restaurants and listened to the sound of waves in the distance. The trip felt peaceful and adventurous at the same time, making Fire Island a place I’d love to visit again. 🌅🌊
